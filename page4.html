Foldable Phones (2019-2025)

← Back

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold (2019)

Foldable phones brought back the flip design but with flexible OLED screens. These devices offered larger displays in a compact form factor, improved multitasking, and new design innovations.

The Samsung Galaxy Fold was Samsung's first foldable phone, featuring a 7.3-inch flexible AMOLED screen and a secondary external display for quick access when folded.

Huawei Mate X (2019)

Huawei entered the foldable phone market with the Mate X, which featured an outward-folding design, eliminating the need for a secondary display.

It had a 6.6-inch display when folded and an 8-inch screen when fully unfolded, offering a sleek and futuristic look.

Motorola Razr (2020)

Motorola revived its iconic Razr flip phone in a modern foldable form, featuring a 6.2-inch flexible OLED display and a nostalgic clamshell design.

It was one of the first foldables to bring a compact and pocketable design, combining nostalgia with modern technology.

Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 3 (2021)

The Galaxy Z Flip 3 improved upon its predecessor with a more durable hinge, a stronger ultra-thin glass display, and an expanded external screen.

It became one of the first mainstream foldables to reach mass adoption, thanks to its affordable price and stylish design.

Oppo Find N2 (2022)

Oppo's Find N2 featured a compact foldable design with minimal screen creases and an innovative hinge mechanism.

It offered a refined folding experience, reducing the gap between the folded display while keeping a lightweight and portable form.

Google Pixel Fold (2023)

Google’s first foldable, the Pixel Fold, introduced a book-style folding mechanism with Pixel-exclusive software optimizations.

It provided a smooth multitasking experience with deep AI integration and Google’s signature computational photography.

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 (2024)

The Galaxy Z Fold 6 introduced a lighter and more durable hinge mechanism, an even thinner form factor, and a nearly crease-free foldable display.

Samsung improved battery efficiency and multitasking capabilities, making it the best productivity-focused foldable device of its time.
